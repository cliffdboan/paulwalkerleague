League Rules

Essential information every member should know

Draft Format

Snake draft, 12 teams, 3 rounds (keeper draft). Draft order determined by random draw.

Scoring

PPR scoring system. 1 point per reception, 6 points per TD, standard yardage bonuses.

Roster

QB, RB, RB, WR, WR, TE, FLEX, K, DEF, 6 Bench spots, 1 IR spot.

Waivers

FAAB waivers with $100 budget, processed Tuesdays at 2 PM EST.

Keepers

Each team may keep 1 player. Playoffs start week 15 with 6 teams.

Prizes

  • 1st place: $660
  • 2nd place: $340
  • 3rd place: $100
